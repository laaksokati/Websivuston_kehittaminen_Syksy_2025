Contact Us

We’re always happy to hear from you. Whether you have questions about adoption, want to learn more about our work, or are interested in volunteering or supporting us, don’t hesitate to get in touch.

Adoption inquiries

If you’re interested in adopting one of our dogs, we recommend starting by filling out the adoption application. If you have questions before applying, feel free to contact us, we’re here to help you make the right decision.

General questions

Have questions about our organization, cooperation with shelters, or our adoption process? We’re happy to answer and provide more information.

Volunteering & support

We’re always grateful for people who want to help. If you’re interested in volunteering, fostering, or supporting our work in other ways, please reach out and tell us a little about yourself.

Response time

We do our best to respond to all messages within a few days. Please note that our team is made up largely of volunteers, so during busy periods replies may take a little longer. Thank you for your understanding.
